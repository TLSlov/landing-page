Who is Joe Joeman?
Our marvelous hero, Joe Joeman, was once the weakest man in Skyrim and he has an ambitious goal. To become the High king of Skyrim. Join him on his (mis)adventures with Faendal, Meeko and flavor of the week horse. Joe is not the Dragonborn, he's just an average joe, he has to eat, drink, sleep, bathe and survive harsh environment of Skyrim. He has no natural magical talents, starting with all skills at level one and he can't learn shouts.
